Paras Healthcare Pvt. Ltd. is a chain of hospitals offering specialized tertiary medical care. The first Paras Hospital was established in Gurgaon in 2006. Over the years, the healthcare organization has established its specialty centers in Chandigarh, Panchkula, Darbhanga, and Patna, with a total capacity of 730 beds.[1] Paras Healthcare was founded by Dr. Dharminder Nagar. The organization is broadly categorized into Paras Hospitals and Paras Bliss, both focusing on respective specialized medical facilities. On June 3, 2019, Paras Healthcare announced the appointment of Puneet Srivastava as its Vice President.[2] Paras Hospitals in Gurgaon, Patna, and Darbhanga offer multispecialty tertiary care, while Paras Bliss in Panchkula and New Delhi focuses on specialized mother and child care. paras Global Hospital Darbhanga Paras HMRI Hospital Patna
